Dance styles

Ballet Dance

Ballet classes are a popular type of dance class, especially for people who are looking for a timeless and classic style. Ballet is a graceful and elegant practice; therefore, these classes are great to learn discipline. This style can be difficult to master as it is highly technical. However, there are certain styles of ballet such as ‘ballet fusion’ which combines other genres. This is done by combining the traditional elements of ballet with some modern elements. These classes are also a great choice for people who are looking to keep fit. This is because the technical elements require a great deal of strength training.

Jazz Dance

Jazz is a fun and high paced genre. Jazz classes tend to be dynamic and fun. This is because the style tends to fuse together a number of elements. The result is a modern and high energy class. This makes it a great type of dance for people who are looking for an energizing challenge.

Contemporary Dance

Contemporary dance is similar in nature to jazz classes. Just like jazz classes, contemporary dance classes tend to feel a bit more relaxed. Contemporary dance is a style that combines energy, style and technical skills. These classes also tend to be very high paced.

Tap Dance

Tap Dance can be a difficult skill to conquer. That’s because tap dance takes plenty of control and co-ordination. To conquer these skills, it will need to be practiced regularly. Therefore, these classes are best for people who are willing to dedicate time to honing the skill. You will also learn how to express yourself solely through the movement of your feet. This will result in a better understanding of rhythms and a better control of your movements.

Hip-Hop Dance

Another popular type of dance class is the Hip-Hop genre, which tends to be very high paced and energetic. These classes are great for anyone looking for something motivating to do. There are many different styles within the hip-hop genre. Therefore, it is best to begin with a beginner’s class. That way you can see which style is right for you.

Swing Dance

Swing dance is a genre of dance that requires a partner. This style of dance takes influence from jazz. This type of dance is also highly athletic as it tends to involve both partners jumping, spinning and swinging.

Belly Dance

Belly dancing originated in the Middle East. This type of dance is a fast emerging with more people choosing to take classes for it. This style of dance is solely focused on the stomach and hips. This makes it a great choice for people who are wanting to tone their stomachs. As well, the class will teach people control and rhythm. The dance will teach people how to shimmy their torso and control their hips. This is also a great alternative to many other styles of dance that are heavily focused on footwork.

Ballroom Dance

Ballroom dance is another popular type of dance class that requires a partner. This style of dance tends to be highly technical. These classes are a great way for people to learn control, and co-ordination. Although the technical moves can be difficult to master. Common practice of this skill can result in improved athletic abilities.

Modern Dance

Modern dance was initially born out of dislike for the limitations of traditional dances such as ballet, which often oppressed dancers with rigid rules and techniques. Currently, modern dance is enjoyed across the world with participants having the ability to practice ballet dancing without having to place strict focus on their techniques or turnout. Instead, dancers can choose a piece of music and use unconventional movements to convey emotions or to tell a meaningful story.
